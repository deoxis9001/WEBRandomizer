Legal notice

ROM handling

This service never receives, stores, or processes any ROM file. All ROM handling (validation, patch application, generation of the .bps patch and the patched .gba file) is performed exclusively in your browser, locally. No copy of your ROM ever passes through our server.

The server only computes, from a seed and settings (setting string / cosmetics string), a list of bytes to modify in a vanilla ROM. This list is sent back to your browser, which applies it locally to your ROM (which you provide yourself).
